// roster

Artists

The Liminal Manifold roster. Musicians, writers, and illustrators who share a common obsession with thresholds, margins, and the spaces in between.

// Musicians

musician

Andrew Noice

DJ and producer of dark ambient and cyberpunk soundscapes. Hour-long mixes for deep focus, night drives, and coding sessions. Frequencies for the sleepless city.
musician

Pudgy Cat

Pudgy Cat crafts atmospheric electronic soundscapes that blur the line between ambient drone and glitchy IDM. A hypnotic blend of lo-fi textures, broken beats, and spectral melodies from the Great Beyond. Also a prolific blogger and general-purpose cat with opinions.
musician

Unlisted Specimens

Unlisted Specimens is an experimental electronic project dwelling in the cracks between ambient, dubstep, and lo-fi. Inspired by Burial, Tomora, and the atmospheric anonymity of city nights. Music for empty parking structures at 3 AM.

// Writers & Illustrators

writer

Beatrix Cross

Beatrix Cross writes at the intersection of horror and desire. Her fiction explores cannibalism, obsession, and the monsters we become when we stop apologizing. Not for the faint of heart.
writer

Don't Hug Me, I'm Reddit

Satirist, absurdist, and reluctant spokesperson for deprecated realities. Member of a collective that should probably not be named. His writing reads like tech support tickets from the end of the universe.
writer

K.T. Hoshi

K.T. Hoshi writes cozy science fiction about found families, small businesses in space, and the stubborn belief that food is love made tangible.
writer

Ximena Castillo Vega

Ximena Castillo Vega writes historical literary fiction set in colonial Latin America. Her work explores institutional corruption, cultural genocide, and the persistence of truth through centuries of silence.
illustrator

Zara Pixel

Zara Pixel is a digital artist and illustrator specializing in book covers, album artwork, and visual identities. Her work blends photorealistic rendering with cinematic lighting, creating images that feel like stills from films that were never made.
