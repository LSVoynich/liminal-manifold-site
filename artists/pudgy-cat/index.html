Musician

Pudgy Cat

Pudgy Cat crafts atmospheric electronic soundscapes that blur the line between ambient drone and glitchy IDM. A hypnotic blend of lo-fi textures, broken beats, and spectral melodies from the Great Beyond. Also a prolific blogger and general-purpose cat with opinions.

Website Spotify YouTube Instagram TikTok X Discord

About Pudgy Cat

pudgycat.io is his home base, where he blogs and shares things. Check in whenever.

On the music side, the debut full-length Eat, Sleep, Dream, Repeat is out now on all streaming platforms. Six tracks of narcotic ambient electronics, tracing the arc of a single night from the first slip of consciousness through the architecture of dreams and back again. Available everywhere via pudgycat.io.

In the works: two haiku collections, Catnip & Chaos and Mayhem & Meows, currently in progress. Short-form, sharp, and written by a cat who has been paying close attention.

Sound

Part ambient meditation, part rhythmic experiment, part accidental noise art. Drawing from Boards of Canada, Burial, and Tim Hecker, the project builds layered compositions that reward patience. Each track is its own liminal space: familiar and strange, comforting and unsettling in roughly equal measure.

Follow

Everything is at pudgycat.io. Music, blog, social links, the whole thing.
